Katsu Curry Sauce

Create a delicious katsu curry sauce recipe that is the perfect accompaniment for crispy breaded chicken, rice and prawn crackers

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp groundnut or vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 5 whole garlic cloves, peeled
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tbsp medium/mild curry powder
  • 600ml chicken stock
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a small pan.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and saut√© for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the carrots and sweat the vegetables slowly for 10 minutes with the lid on, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the flour, curry powder, garam masala and cook for a minute.
  5. Slowly pour in the stock bit by bit until combined (do this gradually to avoid getting lumps).
  6. Add the honey and soy sauce and bring to the boil
  7. Reduce the heat to a slow simmer and cook for 20 minutes (the sauce will have thickened and taken on all of the flavours, although you still need it to have a pouring consistency).
  8. Pass the sauce through a sieve for a chunkier sauce or blend for a smoother sauce.
