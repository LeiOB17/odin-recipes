Kinder Pain Au Chocolat

Follow this quick and easy recipe to bake your own delicious Kinder Pain Au Chocolate!

Ingredients

  • 1 puff pastry roll
  • 12 Kinder Bars
  • Milk/egg to glaze
  • Oil/butter for greasing

Baking steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180
  2. Line a baking tray with baking paper and grease with a little oil/butter
  3. Unroll the puff pastry sheet
  4. Using a pizza wheel cutter, slice the sheet into 6 squares
  5. Place two Kinder bars on each square opposite each other
  6. Fold over each end containing the Kinder bar and then fold again
  7. Place onto the baking tray and glaze
  8. Bake in the oven for 20 mins
